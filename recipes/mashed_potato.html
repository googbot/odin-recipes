Mashed Potato

Big ol mashy taters ferda

  • 1x Russet Tater
  • 1x Tbsp butter
  • 1x cup shredded cheddar
  • 1x splash of milk
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Cut the tater in half
  2. Boil for ~15 minutes (until fork tender) then drain water
  3. Add butter, splash of milk, and shredded chedda to that biznitch
  4. Crack some salt and pepper into there if ya want
