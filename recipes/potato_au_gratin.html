Potato Au Gratin

Creamy sliced taters ferda

  • 1x Russet Tater
  • 5x Tbsp butter
  • 1x Cup Shredded Cheddar
  • 6oz Milk
  • 2 Tbsp Flour
  • 1x Cup Shredded Gruyere Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Cut the tater in half, then use a knife or mandollin to thinly slice your potato
  2. In a sauce pan, make a light cream sauce using the butter, flour, and milk
  3. Add both shredded cheesess to the cream sauce
  4. Crack some salt and pepper into there if ya want
  5. Add thinly sliced taters to an oven safe dish and cover in the cream sauce
  6. Cover ovensafe dish with tin foil and bake for 20 minutes
  7. Remove tin foil, and bake for 5-10 more minutes
